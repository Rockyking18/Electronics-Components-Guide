Resistor

A passive electrical component that limits or regulates the flow of electrical current. Resistors are fundamental to circuit design, controlling voltage and current levels across a wide range of applications.

Uses
  • Current limiting
  • Voltage division
  • Signal attenuation
  • Pull-up/down
Projects
  • LED driver circuits
  • Sensor interfaces
  • Amplifier biasing

Zener Diode

A special type of diode designed to allow current to flow in the reverse direction when the voltage exceeds a specified value — the Zener voltage. Used extensively in voltage regulation.

Uses
  • Voltage regulation
  • Overvoltage protection
  • Reference voltage
  • Clipping circuits
Projects
  • Power supply regulators
  • Signal clampers

LED — Light Emitting Diode

A semiconductor light source that emits light when current flows through it. Electrons recombine with electron holes, releasing energy as photons. Highly efficient, durable, and available in many wavelengths.

Uses
  • Indicator lights
  • Display backlights
  • Lighting
  • Communication (IR)
Projects
  • LED matrix displays
  • Traffic light simulators
  • Mood lighting

Crystal Oscillator

An electronic oscillator circuit that uses the mechanical resonance of vibrating crystal — commonly quartz — to create an electrical signal with a precise frequency. Essential for timing.

Uses
  • Clock generation
  • Microcontroller timing
  • RF transmitters
  • Digital filters
Projects
  • Real-time clocks
  • Digital frequency meters

Transformer

A passive electrical device that converts alternating current from one voltage to another using electromagnetic induction. Essential for power distribution and isolation in circuits.

Uses
  • Voltage conversion
  • Power distribution
  • Electrical isolation
  • Impedance matching
Projects
  • Power supply units
  • Signal couplers

Battery

A device that stores chemical energy and converts it into electrical energy. Comes in various types including alkaline, lithium, and rechargeable variants, each suited for different applications.

Uses
  • Power source

  • Backup power
  • Energy storage
